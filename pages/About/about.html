We have made an impact in the Education industry with our proven knowledge and expertise in the abroad studies. As a leading study abroad consultant, we are 100% committed to the success of the students who are approaching us for getting the much-needed consultation for their study abroad dreams. That’s how we became the best choice for students and job seekers across the state.

Pingme Study Abroad is a subsidiary of the leading travel consultancy PingmeTravels Private Limited. Our team includes the best professionals and industry leaders in terms of knowledge and experience. Our fully equipped team of overseas job consultants in Kerala are always ready to assist the job seekers and students for all types of abroad studies and jobs. From the application to the successful accommodation at the destination country, our company stands with our customers and help them in each step.

Our team of passionate experts curates the best possible career options for job seekers based on their skills and qualifications. For every single application, we put our maximum effort to help the job seekers to land on their dream job with continuous counselling and consulting. For the students, we have a dedicated team for assisting with all study abroad needs. We strive to admit talented individuals to the best courses offered by reputed international universities.
