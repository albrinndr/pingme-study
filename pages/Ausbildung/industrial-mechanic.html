Industrial Mechanic Ausbildung

The industrial mechanic ausbildung program is a 3-year course where you will work in a Germany company as a trainee and attend classes at a vocational school to improve your theoretical knowledge on the topic.

Germany is presently facing a shortage of skilled labour across a lot of industries. Because of this, aspiring individuals from other countries are welcomed to join the ausbildung programs, where they will receive proper training to join the German workforce as skilled labourers. Get in touch with us to become a skilled professional in the field of industrial mechanics in Germany.

Minimum Academic Qualification : Plus-two

German Language Proficiency : B1/B2 Complete Modules

Tuition Fees : None

Duration : 3 Years

Stipend : Available, around €800/month in first year

The most attractive aspect of the industrial mechanic ausbildung program in Germany is that there are no tuition fees. Instead, as part of your employment at the training company, you will receive monthly stipends of close to 800 euros. You will receive the stipend from your first month in Germany itself and the amount will increase on a yearly basis during your course.

Proficiency in the German language is a crucial requirement for joining an IT ausbildung program. Applicants must have a B2 level of German language skills, as the communication at work and the training place will be mostly in German.

Some employers might even accept applicants with a B1 level of proficiency after a language assessment interview.
