Hotel Specialist Ausbildung

If you are interested in pursuing a career in Hotel Management and wish to study abroad at a top-tier institution, then Germany's renowned Ausbildung program offers an excellent opportunity for you.

In German, Ausbildung refers to an apprenticeship program, which includes attending classes at an institute and receiving training at a German hotel establishment. The course usually takes three years to complete.

Minimum Academic Qualification : Plus-two

German Language Proficiency : B1/B2 Complete Modules

Tuition Fees : None

Duration : 3 Years

Stipend : Available, around €800/month in first year

Hotel specialists are employed in different sectors of a hotel and are true all-rounders. They are in charge of most tasks and processes across the hotel, from welcoming guests at the desk to taking reservations, compiling deals and settlements, to accounting and HR work.

Here, students will be attending classes at an institute as well as working and receiving training at a German hotel. In Germany, an Ausbildung in Hotel Specialist offers a unique combination of education and workplace training, setting the stage for a successful career in hospitality and hotel management.

To join an ausbildung program, it is important to have at least B1 level of German language skills. Applicants are required to clear full modules of at least German B1 level. Most of the communication at work place and the training institute will be in German.

Ausbildung programs in Germany are tuition-free. This means that you don’t have to pay any fees to join an ausbildung program in Hotel management.

As part of your apprenticeship, you will be working at a hotel. For this work, you will be paid a monthly stipend. You can meet living expenses with this stipend. As a result, you will not be required to provide additional proof of financing in the form of a blocked account or show money.
