Your education or experience certificates should be translated to the German language to submit it to the German authorities. It is a mandatory requirement, and you need to complete English to German document translation to get approval for various education requests.

However, you can’t randomly choose a service provider for translating your certificates into the German language. You will have to find an authorized German translation services provider for translating your important certificates. Your translated certificates are valid only if a certified and authorized translator provided the translation service. English to German document translation services can be undertaken by a translator sworn in or certified in Germany. The respective embassy won’t recommend a translating service in any case.
