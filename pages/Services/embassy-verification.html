Embassy Verification

None of your Indian documents and certificates is valid for German authorities. You will have to get your documents and certificates verified and approved by the German Embassy to use them in Germany and similar countries.

All public documents issued by the Indian officials are not valid in Germany, which includes your birth certificate, marriage certificate, school certificates, and more. The German Embassy verified each of the documents with the issuing authority and approves them for use in Germany. You need it as mandatory for all foreign purposes such as study abroad, jobs, family reunion, etc.
