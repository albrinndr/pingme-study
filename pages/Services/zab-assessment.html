ZAB Assessment

You might be new to this thing, but ZAB assessment is very critical for higher education and jobs in Germany. It is very important to pass the ZAB assessment to apply for higher education or a job in Germany.

ZAB is referred to as Central Office for Foreign Education, which compares German and non-German educational systems to ensure quality standards and eligibility for certifications and educational background of students and job seekers. For every country, ZAB compares the educational systems to determines which educational certificates entitle to which kind of study in Germany.

You will have to go through a complex application process and pay a verification fee to get the verification. After receiving your documents, it will take around 3 months to process your application and to determine the comparability. They process over 46,000 applications each year, and it makes the process much longer.
