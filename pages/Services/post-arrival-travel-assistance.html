Post-arrival Travel Assistance

You are busy with making schedules and arranging things for your travel to the foreign university or employer. You are halfway there if you have attended our pre-departure guidance program. But that’s not enough; you need to get to know what to expect after landing there. For that, we have created a post-arrival assistance service too.

Ed Euro is there for you at the destination airport with travel assistance after your landing. Our expert counsellors provide you with the list of available tried and tested resources for travel and accommodation to maximize your experience. It includes airport pickups, accommodation arrangements, insurance support, medical provisions, and the support for part-time jobs. That’s one of the unique services we are providing in the entire state for our beloved clients.

Before you fly off, we are committed to ensuring that there will be proper arrangements to receive you at the airport. We will also drop you at your chosen accommodation so that you don’t feel lost in the new country. We give you proper information on the best possible accommodation options considering the distance from your institution and other facilities. Our travel assistance will make you feel more comfortable in a very new country.
